“The hardest thing is not to paint, but to look at the world from another angle."
When I started to draw, I found that the rose I see contains purple, and the sunlight can add some green and dark blue to her. Many philosophers support the conclu that the best theory of colour is a form of anti-realism and they believe that color is entirely a psychological structure, not a physical feature of the world.

So, how do I know my red is your red? What if what I call blue, you call red, and what I call red, you call yellow?

科学家不创造任何东西，而是揭示自然界中现成的隐藏着的真实，艺术家创造真实的类似物。

About me

I'm a business student and I am lucky enough to live in various corners of the world due to my study. Living in different countries has taught me about life and I learned to experience the world while finding myself. Reading, drawing, photography are some of my hobbies, and I have been teaching myself HTML/CSS/Javascript/Python using online resources.

Why I built this website?

I used to be a liberal arts student, you could say that I read a lot, and I enjoy taking notes while reading. My goal this year is to study the Bible, for I found it helps me grow in wisdom and makes me a person more “three-dimensional.

However, the articles I posted on the most popular social media platforms in China have all been deleted due to tight government control on religious issues, so I decided to build this website from scratch to showcase my reading notes, drawings, photos I took during my trips and from my daily life, and my thoughts.

Hope you enjoy it!
